# Roadmap

Planned features and ideas for Terrible-Traffic.

## Add More Intersection Types

- T-intersections (3-way)
- Multi-lane intersections with turn lanes
- Railroad crossings with timed gates
- Merge/diverge zones (on-ramps, off-ramps)
- 4 way stop
- 3 way stop
- 4 way intesection where only one direction stops

## Add a Gaming Mode

- Score-based play with objectives (minimize congestion, maximize throughput)
- Time-limited challenges
- Unlockable road configurations
- Leaderboard or personal bests

## Add Interactivity

- Click to place/remove cars
- Click a car to trigger braking
- Draw custom roads on the canvas
- Build roads and intersections interactively
- Adjust signal timing by clicking signal dots
- Drag cars to reposition them mid-simulation
- Add routing and destinations so cars navigate to goals
- Hover over a car to see live stats (speed, gap, acceleration)

## Features

- Multiple lane roads and intersections (parallel lanes, lane changes, turn-lane assignments)

## Traffic Behaviors

- Pedestrians at crosswalks that pause vehicle flow
- Emergency vehicles that cars yield to, with sirens
- Road crashes/incidents that create real bottlenecks and require clearance
- Highway on/off ramps and merging zones
- Parking lots with entry/exit flow

## Visuals & UI

- Road markings (lane lines, stop lines, crosswalk stripes)
- Day/night cycle with lighting changes
- Mini-map overview for larger road networks

## Driver Personalities

- Per-car IDM parameter variation for realistic heterogeneous traffic
- Dynamic personality slider that shifts the population distribution live
- Selectable presets: Calm, Mixed, Aggressive, Distracted
- Visual indicator per car reflecting its personality type

## Dynamic Traffic

- Rush hour: spawn rate ramps up and down on a configurable time curve
- Time-of-day traffic patterns (morning/evening peaks, midday lull)
